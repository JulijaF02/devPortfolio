My quick story

I've always been drawn to building things – whether it was tree houses as a kid or tearing apart old phones just to understand how all the pieces fit together. My dad introduced me to technology early on, and I became fascinated by how things work, especially games. I'd spend hours wondering how developers made characters move and worlds come alive.

That curiosity naturally led me to coding. The idea that I could create anything I imagined with just code was genuinely exciting. I started with a simple platformer in Stencyl, then taught myself C# in middle school. When I discovered Unity, it felt like the perfect fusion of creativity and technical problem-solving.

I've worked in photography, video editing, and IT engineering, but software and game development is where I really found my element. There's something satisfying about working in fast-paced environments where you can take an idea from concept to a fully working product. The creative problem-solving and the ability to bring ideas to life through code – that's what keeps me motivated.

As a developer

My coding ethos is centered on clarity and accessibility. Initially, I build to ensure functionality, but I dedicate considerable effort to refining my code - commenting, organizing, and ensuring it's welcoming to any collaborator. Mi casa es tu casa in the world of code.

Efficiency and maintainability are my mantras. Functional features are good, but sustainable, and adaptable code is the gold standard. It's not just about making it work; it's about crafting it to last and be easily managed.

I thrive on new projects, as each one is a new chapter of learning and growth. Every challenge is an opportunity to expand my horizons.

As a person

I'm naturally curious and hands-on. I don't just want to know how things work – I need to take them apart, understand the mechanics, and rebuild them. Whether it's hardware, software, or solving complex problems, I approach everything with that same exploratory mindset that had me dismantling phones as a kid.

My drive comes from possibility. When I see a challenge or envision something new, I get excited about the endless ways to approach it. That combination of technical curiosity and creative problem-solving shapes how I tackle everything, from coding to collaboration.

As a colleague

My collaborative spirit is a core part of who I am. With a background encompassing both engineering and the arts, I bridge gaps between the technical and creative minds in any team. Going the extra mile for my team is second nature to me.

With a decade of experience as a teacher, my communication skills are top-notch. I excel at breaking down complex ideas into understandable segments, ensuring everyone is on the same page.

My time at startups, and leading projects with tech giants like Facebook and Amazon, has honed my ability to balance business acumen with high-quality production. My leadership in these collaborations has been a highlight of my career.

I'm always up for making a great espresso and showing off my latte art skills. A food enthusiast at heart, I'm your go-to for the next potluck.

I'm eager to connect and collaborate – reach out!
